Lab 4 - Pseudocoding and Problem-solving

Challenge

Learning to use pseudocode in order to faciliate a coding workflow.

Problems

Nothing specific to report. It was a little difficult to think about real world actions in the form of pseudocode; it was difficult to enumerate tasks into a one-after-the-other format.

Reflection

This kind of activity is actually really helpful to understand how computers actually work, computers can't really do multiple actions at once (at least not in simple programs) and so even in cases where it would make sense in real life to do tasks concurrently, we have to find a way to make those tasks seem concurrent while actually doing them one after the other.
